Lemonade

Description

Ingredients

For creating the cool glass of lemonade pictured here, please collect the following items:

  • Pitcher
  • Spoon
  • Water
  • Lemons
  • Sugar

Steps

Perform the following steps in order:

  1. Fill the pitcher 2/3 full with water
  2. Squeeze 2 lemons worth of juice into the pitcher
  3. Add a half cup of sugar
  4. Stir with the spoon
  5. Drink and enjoy the lemonade
