PB and J

Description

Ingredients

For creating the yummy PB and J pictured here, please collect the following items:

  • Knife
  • Spoon
  • Plate
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly (Grape, Strawberry, or any other flavor- get creative!
  • Sliced Bread

Steps

Perform the following steps in order:

  1. Take two slices of bread and lay them side by side
  2. Using the knife, spread peanut butter on one side of the left slice of bread
  3. Using the spoon, spread jelly on one side of the right slice of bread
  4. Take the slices in each hand, and mush them together, with the condiments facing inward
  5. Eat and enjoy the sandwich
