PB and J

Description

Ingredients

For creating the yummy PB and J pictured here, please collect the following items:

  • Bowl
  • Spoon
  • Greased cookie sheet
  • Cookie Dough
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

Perform the following steps in order:

  1. Combine the dough and chocolate chips in the bowl
  2. Mix well with spoon
  3. Using the spoon, create ~1 inch size balls and add them to greased cookie sheet
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for 7-10 minutes or until golden brown
  5. Eat and enjoy the cookies
